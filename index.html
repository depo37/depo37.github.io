Life Below Water

Quick Facts

  • 75% of Earth is covered by the ocean and 99% of living space
  • Over 3 billion people rely on the ocean to drive global cycles
  • 40% of the ocean has been heavily affected by pollution
  • 200,000 species have been identified in the ocean but over million may exist
  • 30% of carbon dioxide is absorbed by the ocean contributing to global warming
